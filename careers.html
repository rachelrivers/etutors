WORK WITH US

Interested in joining our team?

The eTutors are working hard to support students and families from all backgrounds as they transition to remote learning. We want to make sure students have the support they need so they won't fall behind in the virtual school setting. We also help parents who may not be up to date with the current technologies with webinars and one on one sessions.

What we are looking for team members who connect with our cause:

  • People who are passionate about education, and want to take on the challenge of helping students not stray behind – we are working to close any opportunity gaps or disadvantages students may face by having to attend school remotely.
  • People who are excited to have responsibilities and are willing to work on projects outside their regular duties if needed.
  • Are you a people person? We need team members who excel at communication and are willing to go above and beyond to connect with our families.

Not quite ready to switch careers? Check out our volunteering opportunities if you still are interested in helping!
