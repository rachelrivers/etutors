The role 'Approach & Environment' play in the Learning process

The truth is that when we scrub joy and comfort from the classroom, we distance our students from effective information processing and long-term memory storage. Instead of taking pleasure from learning, students become bored, anxious, and anything but engaged. They ultimately learn to feel bad about school and lose the joy they once felt. eTutors is dedicated to placing the fun back into the learinig environment with the use of relevence based teaching methods, along with the many other proven helpful learning tools to ensure a successful and enriching experience for each student.

Game-based learning has the potential to significantly enhance a student's level of engagement

It is not clear whether the positive effects of game-based learning stem from greater time spent learning, or increased efficiency in learning, or both. It is clear, however, that more time is spent learning when educational games are used than when they are not. Tobias et al report that those who learn using games, “tend to spend more time on them than do comparison groups.” Here at eTutors, we're putting that theory to the test by offering our students the opportunity to test and even build upon their knowledge while having fun at our Eureka Center!
