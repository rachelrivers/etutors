VISION AND MISSION

Vision

eTutors helps students by implementing learning objectives that improve self-sufficiency with online learning, as well as promote enjoyment and success while you learn!

Mission

eTutuors mission is to provide a safe, comfortable and supportive environment that stimulates intellectual growth for students who are utilizing academic services. The ultimate goal is to stimulate students in developing successful habits in their studies and enjoy learning.
